Our Wedding

Our dear family and friends,

We're getting married! We don’t want a big to-do but rather a gathering of our most cherished people for a weekend of unity, revelry and celebration of love. We've reserved a beautiful farm estate that has enough accommodation for 40 people and we hope that you will stay with us for the full duration of the weekend.

Itinerary

Friday February 1st: Casual potluck evening
Saturday February 2nd: General milling around, leading up to sunset ceremony and banquet dinner

Victuals

The venue is about 30mins away from either of the two small towns that it sits between so we’ve made some efforts to make sure that everyone is provided for over the weekend:

If you will be joining us for the meet-and-greet potluck on Friday night; just bring a small dish of anything that tickles your fancy. We will create a chat group at a later stage to coordinate these efforts.

If you’re paying for accommodation at our venue, the owners will provide you with a breakfast on the Saturday and Sunday mornings.

There will be a banquet dinner on Saturday night but we will provide snacks to tide you over from breakfast.

There will be a cash bar available for the duration of weekend. We will request that they stock the bar with wine, beer and whiskey but if you have any personal preferences then feel free to let us know and we'll make sure they have it available. The owners will charge a corkage fee of R40 (USD3 NT90) per bottle.
